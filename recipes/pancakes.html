Pancake

Description

Everyone loves pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¼ cups powdered milk
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Steps

  1. Combine flour, powdered milk, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl; stir to blend.
  2. Pour onto hot pan and cook both sides until golden.
  3. Serve and enjoy!
